Projects I'm proud of

Software DevelopmentCollaborative Project2023

LinuxPedia

Linuxpedia is an Augmented Reality project designed to educate users about the open-source Linux operating system. The project aims to provide an interactive and engaging learning experience, enhancing users' understanding and knowledge of Linux through innovative AR technology.

Internet of ThingsCollaborative Project2023

FantastiCAM

Fantasticam is a CCTV project based on the ESP32-CAM, designed to enhance security in various environments. This project aims to provide a flexible and accessible solution for monitoring and protecting surroundings, utilizing the capabilities of the ESP32-CAM to ensure comprehensive surveillance and safety.

Internet of ThingsCollaborative Project2024

Hy-Scape

Hy-Scape is a device designed to control temperature, pH levels, and provide feeding for aquascapes through Telegram. This project offers a comprehensive and automated solution for maintaining optimal conditions in aquascapes, ensuring the health and well-being of aquatic life through seamless integration with Telegram for remote monitoring and control.

Internet of ThingsIndividual Project2025

Lock Face

Lock Face is an Internet of Things (IoT) based smart security system designed to enhance access control using face recognition technology. The system utilizes an ESP32-CAM module, a compact microcontroller equipped with a camera and built-in Wi-Fi capability, to detect and recognize human faces in real time.
